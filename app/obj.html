Lot Essay

Ammonites are cephalopods that were widespread during the Mesozoic era. Since the Devonian period and for three hundred million years, they have played an important role in marine ecosystems. The ammonite from our collection was found in Madagascar, in Jurassic sediments. The age of the exhibit is 163 - 145 million years. The shell has a spectacular appearance, its surface is covered with large transverse ribs. In some places, under a thin layer of fossilized mother-of-pearl, you can see a beautiful lobed line pattern. The condition is good, the shell has not been subjected to additional processing. Ammonite shells are one of the favorite collectibles.
